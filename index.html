About the Lab

Our Mission

We investigate the plasticity of wound healing by integrating behavioral biology, developmental neuroscience, and regenerative physiology. Our long-term goal is to translate insights from highly regenerative species into therapies for humans.

Background

Founded in Fall 2025 by Dr. Justin Varholick, the Regenerative Plasticity Lab builds on a unique blend of behavioral science and regenerative biology. Our current focus explores how post-injury behavior and neuroplasticity shape healing trajectories across development and species.

Our Team

Dr. Justin Varholick

Lab Director

Dr. Varholick holds an M.S. in Biopsychology from UNC Greensboro and a Ph.D. in Biomedical Science/Applied Ethology from the University of Bern. He trained in regenerative biology under Prof. Malcolm Maden at the University of Florida. His work has contributed to establishing behavioral frameworks for studying tissue regeneration in Acomys and mice.
